Summary

Our application combines two seperate API's, one being for the Weather and the other being Events. The idea behind this is that you will go onto TicketMaster (link in the Navigation bar) and browse for an event you wish to attend. You will then enter this event into the search bar of the Weather and Events page, and will be shown what the predicted weather will be for that day.

If you wish to access this on a different device such as a mobile or tablet, there is a download link on the Weather and Events Page.
